How to start

Hello! Written below you will find my definitive guide on how to start Web Development. The purpose of this guide is to help complete beginners find the tools and resources required to start enjoying the wonderful world of web dev. Whether you are here looking for help with making your personal website, or you are seeking a career in web dev, this guide will help show you the ropes on what few seem to mention, the necessary tools and fundamental items required to get going. My goals are to 1. Describe and refer you to the (free) tools necessary to start and efficently work on your project, and 2. Point you to high quality educational resources that will enchance your knowledge and help you with clear, detailed tutorials on solving your specific issues. With all of that out of the way, lets get started!
